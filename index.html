Introduction

Hi, I am Likhita and I hail from Hyderabad. I am currently doing my MBA-IT from Symbiosis Institute of Computer Studies and Research with Data Analytics specializaton. I have completed my under graduation B.Com (Comp) from Little Flower Degree College and my schooling from Kendriya Vidhyalaya. During the campus drive in my degree college I got placed in Wipro and have worked in the IT Sector for around 2 years since then. Check out my Wipro Work Experience details here.

Work Experience

I joined Wipro after I completed my degree. I completed my 3 months training in Greater Noida where they taught us the basics of Wintel, Networking and Unix. After my training I was shifted to Banglore for joining Projects. I had joined the GIS- Competency team as a adhoc member to help with the pending open tickets, but soon I was taken into the team seeing my meticulous work and my ability to learn quickly. After a few of months of joining the team I was given additional responsibilities to handle the role of the Business Analyst for the team and track all the open action items. As part of this role I was also driving the weekly call for the team.

After completing around 1 year with the team and being promoted to A3 band I was given additional responsibility to handle a framework named "3- Dimensional Service Delivery Excellence" which was being implemented across the Business Line. As part of my new role I had to handle 90+ accounts and also track new newly onboarded Transition Accounts. One of the best parts of journey with Wipro was about the connections I got to make across all the 90+ account in Global Infrastructure Structure . I also got to travel a lot between Pune, Hyderabad and Bengaluru for auditing and floor talks.

During my tenure , I was also awarded the GIS- Standing Ovation Award for being able to turn majority of the accounts Compliant. It was a lovely experience with Wipro , looking forward to more challenging responsibilities through out my career.
