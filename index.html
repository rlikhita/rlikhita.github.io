Work Experience

I joined Wipro after I completed my degree. I completed my 3 months training in Greater Noida where they taught us the basics of Wintel, Networking and Unix. After my training I was shifted to Banglore for joining Projects. Due to change in UCG guidlines , we where converted from Student to a Team Rainbow employee within 6 months of joining the company. I had joined the then GIS- Competency team as a adhoc member to help with the pending open tickets, but soon I was taken into the team seeing my ability to learn quickly. After a few of months of joining the team I was given additional responsibilitites to handel the role of the Business Analyst for the team and track all the open action items. As part f this role I was also driving the weekly call for the team.

After completing around 1 year with the team and being promoted to the A3 band I was given additional responsibility to handel a framework called as "3- Dimensional Service Delivery Excellence" which was being implemented across the Business Line. As part of my new role I was handling 90+ accounts and also tracking new newly onboarded Transition Accounts. One of the best parts of journey with Wipro was about the connections I got to make. And I also got to travel a lot between Pune, Hyderabad and Banglore.

During my tenure , I was also awarded the GIS- Standing Ovation Award for being able to turn majority of the accounts Compliant. It was a lovely experience , looking forward to more challenging responsibilitites.
